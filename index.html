RULES & EGULATIONS

  • ■ The 3 day event will be held on HackerRank and each round is 2 hours long.
  • ■ There will be 2 qualifier rounds. Participants can attend only one of the qualifiers. The top 60 will be qualified for the next round.
  • ■ In Round 1 top 30 participants will move to Round 2.
  • ■ In Round 2 top 15 participants will move to the final round
  • ■ In the final round top 3 will be declared as winners based on their scores.
